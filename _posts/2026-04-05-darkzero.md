We enable xp_cmdshell on the linked computer and gain a service account which is missing privileges. We recover credentials and are able to escalate to a service shell to recover our privileges. Gaining back SeImpersonate privileges we can utilize a potato privilege escalation to get Administrator on DC02. Using this access we find our forest trust permits delegation across the trust. We abuse this delegation using our MSSQL access to retrieve a TGT and gain root.
